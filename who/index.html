Eric A. Meyer is a multimedia artist specializing in collaborative creation, writing, and performance. He is a co-founder, designer, and web developer with OddBird; multi-instrumentalist and poet with Teacup Gorilla; and all-around co-sucker with Vicious Trap. He is also the creator of Susy, and a core contributor to Compass.

Formerly Artistic Director of New World Arts and Technical Director of The LIDA Project, Eric is currently developing a clusterfuck opera with Vicious Trap, a multimedia novel with OddBird & Teacup Gorilla, and an open-source queer memory of the body with anyone who is interested.

A selection of Eric’s poetry will be published by EOAGH this spring, in conjunction with their upcoming “Anthology of Trans and Genderqueer Poetry.”
