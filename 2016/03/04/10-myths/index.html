10 Myths at Buntport

March 04, 2016

I’m excited to be part of Buntport Theater’s 40th original full-length production — based on my novel, Riding SideSaddle*, and featuring a live score by Teacup Gorilla. It’s been a wild month pulling it all together!

  • 10 Myths on the Proper Application of Beauty Products
  • Buntport Theater: 717 Lipan St, Denver CO
  • March 4-26, 2016

The first weekend sold out, and the second weekend is going fast. Read the reviews in Out Front, The Denver Post, Huffington Post, and Colorado Drama — then reserve your tickets before they’re gone!

Riding SideSaddle*

a novel | by Miriam Eric Suzanne

This fragmented memory follows a cast of friends as they navigate fluid genders and relationships, with bodies that resist order, category, or completion.
