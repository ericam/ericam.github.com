Hello World:

Eric Meyer, OddBird lead designer, is a multimedia artist with experience in theatre, music, film, writing, design, photography, & illustration. He specializes in collaborative process, leading creative teams in the development of new work.

Formerly the Artistic Director of New World Arts and Technical Director of The LIDA Project, Eric is now the lead designer at OddBird, a musician with Teacup Gorilla writer, producer, and performer with Vicious Trap, creator of the Susy responsive grid framework, and a core developer on the Compass project.

Also on the OddBird design team: Sondra Eisenstat is a marketing consultant, writer, and designer who specializes in brand development; and Aaron Liechty is a writer, performer, and illustrator.

This site is built on plain old semantic HTML5 and styled using CSS3 with help from Modernizr, Susy, Compass, Sass, rgbapng, Lemonade, css-lightbox and css-slideshow.

Interaction is aided by jQuery and the ScrollTo, Viewport and Cycle plugins, including associated scripts by Crush+Lovely, used with permission.

The main serif text face is Century Old Style FS with an ampersand borrowed from Baskerville FS Italic, both © 2010 Font Site and made available for web use by Font Spring.

I also use the freely available Silkscreen by Jason Kottke and Chunk Five by The League of Moveable Type as well as various fonts pulled directly from your device.
