Moving off Tumblr

July 21, 2012 in rstblog, tumblr.

I've enjoyed using tumblr for the most part, but it's difficult to maintain a custom design and get all the details just the way I like them.

Instead, I'm moving to the rstblog static site generator. While I'm at it, I've simplified the design down to a bare minimum.

It may take some time to get all my old posts ported over to the new system. Stay with me. In the meantime, I will try to become more regular in my posting habits, and more diverse in my posting topics.
