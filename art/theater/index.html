Theater

Miriam has been writing, designing, and directing live theater since 2001, when she returned from an internship at Oregon Shakespear Festival to became the first Artistic Director of New World Arts in Goshen, Indiana. Miriam has also been a Technical Director (LIDA Project, Countdown to Zero), Master Electrician (Goshen College), award-winning playwright, and founder of Grapefruit Lab.
