Music

Miriam is a song-writer, vocalist, and multi-instrumentalist providing bass, guitar, and mandolin. Previously a member of Elephants for Sale, and Dirt Circle Dogs, Miriam is currently playing with Teacup Gorilla and Ersatz Robots.
